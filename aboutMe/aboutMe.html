Hi, I'm Sean Jones, a self-taught web-developer with a passion for all things programming. I really enjoy spending my spare time learning new concepts and frameworks through structured, personal learning and project development. I have a good grasp on the basics of front-end web development and I’m keen to continue improving my skill-set. I’m currently learning how to improve the user experience of my projects by studying the different frameworks of Javascript such as React and Node. I’m excited to start a full time position as a developer and gain mentorship from more experienced, passionate seniors.

Outside of work and programming I’m a family man with two children. I love spending my free time with the family, going on exciting days out and doing all the fun things dads get to do. I’m also a member of my local fishing club and try to get out to the lake as much as I can (provided the weather isn't too bad!)
